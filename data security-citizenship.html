  • Data Security/Citizenship

Data security is the process of safeguarding digital information throughoutits entire life cycle to protect it from corruption, theft, or unauthorised access.

It covers everything—hardware, software, storage devices, anduser devices; access and administrative controls; and organizations’ policies and procedures.

Digital citizenship emphasises individual responsibility to participate insociety. It is the attributes that empower by emphasising citizens’ abilities (and responsibilities) to understand, navigate and participate in the digital world.
